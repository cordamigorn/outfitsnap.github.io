Welcome to OutfitSnap ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application OutfitSnap (the "App"). This policy applies to all users of our AI-powered outfit generation and wardrobe management service.

- **Contact Email:** Support@OutfitSnap.app

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Data Collection

| Data Type | Collection Method | Storage Location | Retention Period | Purpose |
|-----------|------------------|------------------|------------------|---------|
| **Email Address** | User Registration | Supabase Authentication | Account Lifetime | User authentication and account management |
| **User Name** | User Registration | Supabase Database | Account Lifetime | Profile identification and personalization |
| **Clothing Images** | User Upload | Supabase Storage | Account Lifetime | Wardrobe management and outfit recommendations |
| **AI-Generated Outfits** | AI Processing | Supabase Storage | Account Lifetime | Outfit recommendations and user history |
| **Subscription Metadata** | Apple In-App Purchases | Apple Systems | Per Apple Policy | Payment processing and subscription management |

### 2.2 Data We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we **do not** collect:
- **Body Photos**: While users can temporarily upload body photos for AI outfit generation, these images are **immediately deleted** after processing and are **never stored** on our servers
- **Credit Card Information**: All payment processing is handled directly by Apple through In-App Purchases
- **Location Data**: We do not collect or track your geographic location
- **Device Identifiers**: We do not collect device IDs, advertising IDs, or similar identifiers
- **Contacts or Phone Numbers**: We do not access your contacts or require phone numbers
- **Browsing History**: We do not track your browsing activity outside the App

### 2.3 Technical Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information:
- App version and usage analytics (anonymized)
- Error logs and crash reports (through our error monitoring service)
- API usage statistics (for service optimization)

## 6. Data Security and Safeguards

### 6.1 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

- **Data Encryption**: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS encryption
- **Secure Authentication**: User passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Security Updates**: Regular updates to our security protocols and systems

### 6.2 Immediate Deletion of Sensitive Data
Body photos uploaded for AI outfit generation are:
- Processed in memory only
- **Never stored** on our servers
- **Immediately deleted** after AI processing is complete
- Used solely for generating outfit recommendations

### 6.3 Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you promptly in accordance with applicable laws.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

### 7.1 Account Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Personal data is retained for as long as your account remains active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Accounts inactive for more than 2 years may be subject to deletion
- **User-Initiated Deletion**: You can delete your account at any time through the App

### 7.2 Account Deletion Process
When you delete your account:
1. All personal data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days
2. Clothing images and generated outfits are permanently removed
3. Account information is irreversibly deleted
4. Some anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement

### 7.3 Legal Retention Requirements
We may retain certain information for longer periods if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).
